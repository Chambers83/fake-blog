About Me

Hi there, my name is Nick and I am 24 years old. Hailing from Lake Tapps, WA, I attended Auburn Riverside High School and went on the University of Washington to earn my B.A. in Sociology. I've always had a love for the outdoors, whether it's longboarding or playing tennis during the summer months or going snowboarding during the winter. One of my favorite things to do rain or shine is going camping, both car and tent, although I have to say backpacking is my all-time favorite. After graduating college, I took a position as an Americorps VISTA with United Way of Pierce County in Tacoma, where I worked to develop and expand their early childhood education programs. After completing my year-long service term with Americorps, I accepted a position with FareStart, a nonprofit in Seattle, as a Catering and Events Captain. Over the course of the last year I have dabbled a bit with codeacademy and thoroughly enjoyed it so far. Having thought about web development as possible career, I decided to enroll in the evening intro to web development course at Coding Dojo to learn the fundamentals and more about the tech industry.
